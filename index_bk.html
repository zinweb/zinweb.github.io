Play Roller Baller Unblocked Game

Description

Roller Baller has a series of stages in which the goal is to guide the golf ball through a series of obstacles to the golden platform at the end. There are a total of 40 stages, and each one adds a new setpiece that presents a distinct difficulty as the number of obstacles rises throughout the game. You must take care not to fall off the level as you go through each level; if you do, you must redo the level.

Once you've completed all 40 levels, you'll be able to play Freeplay mode, where the aim is simply to get as far as you can without falling.

Controls:

  • Use the arrows keys or WASD to move. Press Space to jump. Get across the platforms to the shiny area!
  • Space - Pause / restart
